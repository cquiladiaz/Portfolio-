About me

Hello!

My name is Christian, and I am a Web Developer with experience in creating innovative digital solutions. My main strength is combining my perseverance and technical skills to develop attractive and functional web experiences.

I have worked with technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript to create modern and responsive websites. I am always seeking new ways to improve my work and provide efficient solutions to the challenges that arise…

In addition, I have a strong focus on usability and user experience. I firmly believe that good design should not only be aesthetically pleasing but also intuitive and easy to use. I strive to create interfaces that are accessible and provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for users.

I am excited to be a part of challenging projects and collaborate with talented teams. Since October 2022, I have been working at Hogarth as a Frontend Developer. If you are looking for a passionate and dedicated developer, I would love to talk to you and explore how I can contribute to your project!

Fun facts: I also have a particular interest in nature and aquatic life. As a personal project, I have created a small ecosystem in my living room—an aquarium with different species of fish from the Amazon River. This project allows me to combine my fascination with nature with my ability to create and maintain a balanced environment for these incredible aquatic inhabitants.
